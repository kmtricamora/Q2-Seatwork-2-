The Characters of Noli Me Tangere

Image of the Character Name of the Character Description of the Character
Maria Clara The representation of an ideal Filipina. She is the daughter of Padre Damaso.
Ibarra He is Filipino-Spanish. Son of Don Rafael Ibarra.
Padre Damaso He is the former curate of the parish church of San Diego. He does not control what he says.
Tiya Isabel She helped raised Maria Clara. She also greeted the guests in the first chapter.
Kapitan Tiago He is the only son of a wealthy trader. He hosted a party in the first chapter.
